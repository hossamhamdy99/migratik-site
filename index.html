FAQ

Questions, answered.

Is it safe to run on a live site?

Yes. Migratik is read-only — it only reads your content and hands you a file. It never changes your posts, database, or settings, and leaves nothing behind when deleted.

Which page builders are supported?

Elementor, WPBakery, Divi, Beaver Builder, Bricks, Oxygen, Gutenberg, and the Classic editor. Each item is rendered with the right method and tagged so nothing renders empty.

Do I need to be a developer?

No. Everything happens with a dropdown and a button inside your WordPress admin — pick a format, click download. No command line, no code.

What happens to my images?

With Pro, one click bundles every image and file alongside the export and rewrites the URLs to the packaged copies — including responsive srcset sizes. The result is fully self-contained.

Is this a subscription I’m stuck with?

Your license includes a year of updates and support. Since migration is usually one-time, you can cancel renewal after your migration and keep using the version you have.
